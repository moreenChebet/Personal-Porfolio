About Me

My name is Maurine Chebet. Software developer, graphic designer and technical trainer by proffession.
I train kids & teens on how to code, be safe online, digital literacy and web development.
In this digital age, some parents and caregivers navigate the challenges faced by ignoring them.
This however presents a temporary solution to a permanent problem.
For this case, I chip in by training them on ways of solving those challenges.
I cover various technology related issues that affect our day to day lives with the goals being:

  1. Creating awareness on the benefits of technology
  2. Elaborating on disasters that can result when technology is misused
  3. Online safety
